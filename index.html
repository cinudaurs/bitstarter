The idea for 0Degrees is born from the need for a missed connection based app which reduces the spam and for providing authenticity to the post that can serve as a basis for starting a real communication in a quick and easier way.

Some of the features of The 0Degrees app :

* The idea of attaching a check-in location to a post should limit the number of spam messages people post.

* Even if somebody is going to be posting with random check-in locations just to spam, our idea to limit the post to 140 char limit (twitter style) should make it effective to deal with such spam and thus reduce it.

* You can a like/dislike or favorite a post and this helps in the good posts to float to the top.

* Home feed - The homepage would have a feed of posts sorted by the maximum likes/favorites. the idea of homepage feed which has the best of the posts can have an audience of its own and a 140 char limit on the posts would make them for quick n fun reads.

* Search - to search by geo-location for a missed connection. This geo-location check-in by one of the person in a missed connection would validate to the other that he/she is indeed the person from the missed connection trying to reconnect.

* Post - Post your missed connection and add a location with check-in feature.

* Favorites - List of posts you've favorited. 140 char limit challenges the posts to be witty/funny/heart-warming to catch the attention of any reader. Save the ones you've loved reading.

* Profile - Your profile page. It would require you to signup to post ur memo of the missed connection tagged with a check-in.
